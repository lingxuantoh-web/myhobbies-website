My first hobby is oil pastel painting.Oil pastel painting uses sticks made of pigment mixed with a non-drying oil and wax binder, giving them a creamy, buttery texture similar to both wax crayons and traditional pastels.

smooth, creamy texture, similar to a crayon, that allows for bold, vibrant colors and smooth blending

My Experience

I have been using oil pastels since I was ten years old, during my art class at school and they have become one of my favorite things to do. I like how the colors are soft and bright, and how they can blend together so smoothly. When I draw with them, it feels relaxing, and I can show my feelings through the colors I choose. I enjoy seeing my own art work when it's completed.I like oil pastels because they let me be creative and make art in my own way.

Who inspires me

Vincent van Gogh was a Dutch painter who used bright colors and strong brushstrokes which uses oil pastels in his art. He made many paintings, but he had a hard life and was not famous while he was alive. Some of his most well-known works are The Starry Night and Sunflowers. I like his paintings because they show a lot of feelings and energy. The colors are bold and make the pictures stand out. His art feels honest, and it shows how even simple things can be beautiful
